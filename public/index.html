Please note that only the selected recipient identity can read the note, provided that the recipient have the private-key for the identity.

If you digitally sign the note, the recipient can verify that your identity was used to sign the note.

Create identity

Creating an identity allows you to digitally sign a sent message and receive messages from other users.

Please note that you should never disclose anything personal like your real name or email here.

Your identity has a public and private side. You can safely share your public identity to anybody, but you must keep the private part secret to protect your identity.

If you make this identity exportable, you can share it (manually) with your other computer or device.

Import identity

Importing an private identity allows you to digitally sign a sent message and receive messages from other users. Importing an public identity allows you to send messages to the user.

Please note that you never should disclose anything personal like your real name or email here.

Your private identity has a public and private side. You can safely share your public identity to anybody, but you must keep the private part secret to protect your identity. public identity only has public side.

If you make this identity exportable, you can share it (manually) with your other computer or device.

Export identity

Exporting an identity allows you to use the same identity on some other computer or device.
